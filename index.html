Science Is... is a Calgary, Alberta, Canada owned and operated company. We specialize in science tools, toys, equipment and supplies for all ages. Everything from test tubes and magnifiers to books and magnets. We also carry a selection of science related toys that are perfect for birthdays and other special occasions. We ship pretty well anywhere. We can custom design science kits at the request of teachers and homeschool groups.

The president of Science is..., George Pastirick, began experimenting with science when he was 12yrs old with a "lab" in the basement iof his parents' house. He soon became fascinated with earth science, while tinkering with chemistry, physics, and biology.
